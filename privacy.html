privacy policy

last updated: october 17, 2025

ðŸ”’ the short version

your files never leave your device. everything is processed locally in your browser. we don't collect, store, or transmit your data. period.

how it works

media converter is a client-side application. this means:

  • no file uploads: your images and videos are processed entirely in your browser using javascript and webassembly (ffmpeg.wasm)
  • no server storage: we don't have servers to store your files on
  • no tracking: we don't use analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts
  • no accounts: no login required, no data collection

what data we collect

none. seriously.

we don't collect:

  • personal information
  • uploaded files or converted files
  • ip addresses
  • usage statistics
  • cookies or local storage (except for theme preference)

youtube & instagram downloads

when you use the youtube or instagram downloader:

  • requests are made directly from your browser to youtube/instagram servers
  • we act as a proxy to bypass cors restrictions
  • video/image data flows directly to your device
  • we don't log urls, downloads, or any metadata
  • you're subject to youtube and instagram's terms of service

third-party services

we use:

  • cdn.jsdelivr.net: to load ffmpeg.wasm library (open source video processing)
  • youtube-dl/yt-dlp: for downloading youtube videos (runs on our server, but we don't log anything)
  • instaloader: for downloading instagram media (same as above)

these services may have their own privacy policies.

data security

since your data never leaves your device:

  • there's no data breach risk
  • no unauthorized access possible
  • no data retention issues
  • you have complete control

local storage

we only store:

  • theme preference: dark/light mode setting (stored in browser localstorage)

you can clear this anytime in your browser settings.

offline usage

the file converter works completely offline once loaded. your browser may cache the website files for faster loading. this is standard browser behavior and you control it.

children's privacy

we don't collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. since there's no data collection, coppa compliance is inherent.

gdpr compliance

we're gdpr compliant by design because:

  • no personal data is collected
  • no data processing occurs on our servers
  • no cookies are used (except theme preference)
  • no consent is needed because there's nothing to consent to

your rights

you have complete control over your data because it never leaves your device. you can:

  • clear browser cache to remove cached files
  • disable javascript to prevent the app from running
  • use the app offline
  • close the tab to stop all processing

changes to this policy

if we ever change our privacy practices (unlikely since we don't collect anything), we'll update this page and change the "last updated" date at the top.

open source

our code is open source. you can review exactly how everything works on github.

questions?

if you have questions about privacy, send us feedback.
